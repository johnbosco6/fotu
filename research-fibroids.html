Uterine Fibroids

Epidemiological patterns and health disparities in treatment.

Project Overview

Uterine fibroids are the most common benign tumors in women of reproductive age, yet their exact etiology remains unclear, and their impact is disparately felt across demographic groups. My research investigates the intersection of environmental risk factors, genetics, and socioeconomic determinants in fibroid development and diagnosis delay.
