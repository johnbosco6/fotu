Research Areas

Evidence-based epidemiological research to inform policy and improve clinical care.

My research program sits at the intersection of clinical gynecology and population health. By utilizing large-scale epidemiological data and robust mixed-methods approaches, we aim to uncover the systemic risk factors and long-term consequences of under-researched women's health conditions.

Female Genital Mutilation

Comprehensive studies on the obstetrical, psychological, and physiological consequences of FGM. We evaluate the efficacy of current surgical and psychological interventions to provide holistic care models.

Detailed FGM Research

Uterine Fibroids

Investigating the genetic, environmental, and socioeconomic determinants of uterine fibroids. Our goal is to understand racial disparities in prevalence and develop less invasive management pathways.
