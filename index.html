Advancing Women's Health Through Research

Welcome. I am dedicated to improving health outcomes for women worldwide through rigorous epidemiological research, with a specific focus on female genital mutilation (FGM) and uterine fibroids. My work bridges the gap between scientific inquiry, public health policy, and community well-being.

Core Research Areas

Global Health

Female Genital Mutilation (FGM)

Investigating the long-term health consequences, epidemiological trends, and prevention strategies for FGM to support evidence-based policy and healthcare interventions.

Explore Research
Reproductive Health

Uterine Fibroids

Studying the risk factors, symptomatology, and impacts on quality of life associated with uterine fibroids, with a focus on disparities in diagnosis and treatment.
